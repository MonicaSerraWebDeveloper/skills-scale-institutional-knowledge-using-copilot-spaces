# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **With Technical Lead:** Receives architectural guidance and code review feedback
- **With QA Lead:** Collaborates on acceptance criteria and defect resolution
- **With Product Managers:** Clarifies requirements and acceptance criteria
- **With Project Managers:** Reports progress and identifies blockers

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **With Project Managers:** Align on priorities, dependencies, and timelines
- **With QA Lead:** Define acceptance criteria and quality expectations
- **With UX/Design Lead:** Validate user research and design direction
- **With Stakeholders:** Present roadmap and business impact

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **With Product Managers:** Align on scope and prioritization
- **With Scrum Master:** Coordinate agile ceremonies and team velocity
- **With Technical Lead:** Identify technical risks and dependencies
- **With Stakeholders:** Provide status updates and escalate blockers

---

## QA Lead / Testing Manager

### Role Summary
QA Leads own the quality strategy and testing approach for projects. They define acceptance criteria, design test plans, manage QA resources, and ensure deliverables meet quality standards before release.

### Responsibilities
- Define QA strategy and test approach aligned with project scope
- Collaborate with Product and Project Managers on acceptance criteria
- Design test cases and manage test automation framework
- Lead manual QA and coordinate with developers on defect resolution
- Verify compliance with Definition of Done before sign-off
- Report quality metrics and identify testing risks
- Plan for end-to-end smoke tests before release

### Goals
- Deliver high-quality features that meet acceptance criteria
- Reduce defect escape and improve customer satisfaction
- Enable fast, reliable testing to support delivery cadence

### Typical Communication
- Test planning workshops during backlog refinement
- Daily standups and progress updates
- Quality metrics reviews in weekly delivery syncs
- Release readiness assessments before production deployment

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **With Developers:** Validates implementations, coordinates defect resolution
- **With Product Managers:** Refines acceptance criteria and test cases
- **With Project Managers:** Reports testing status and quality blockers
- **With DevOps:** Coordinates test environment setup and deployment verification

---

## Technical Lead / Solutions Architect

### Role Summary
Technical Leads provide strategic technical guidance and architectural oversight. They conduct design reviews, identify technical risks, and ensure solutions are scalable, maintainable, and aligned with system architecture.

### Responsibilities
- Review technical designs and propose architectural improvements
- Identify technical dependencies and integration risks
- Advise on technology choices and trade-offs
- Participate in code reviews for complex or high-risk changes
- Document technical decisions and rationale
- Coach junior developers on technical best practices
- Assess technical feasibility and effort estimates

### Goals
- Ensure solutions are technically sound and maintainable
- Reduce technical debt and rework
- Enable team learning and capability growth

### Typical Communication
- Design review sessions before implementation
- Architecture decision records and technical memos
- Code review feedback and mentoring
- Technical risk assessments during planning and execution

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **With Developers:** Provides architectural guidance and mentorship
- **With Project Managers:** Highlights technical risks and dependencies
- **With Security/Compliance Officer:** Ensures secure architecture and compliance
- **With DevOps:** Coordinates infrastructure and deployment strategy

---

## UX/Design Lead

### Role Summary
UX/Design Leads own the user experience and design direction for projects. They conduct user research, create design specifications, validate usability, and ensure consistent, user-centered solutions.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and translate insights into design decisions
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Validate design through usability testing and feedback
- Ensure design consistency across products and platforms
- Collaborate on accessibility and user-centered requirements
- Handoff designs to developers with clear specifications

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, user-centered solutions
- Improve user satisfaction and engagement
- Enable smooth design-to-development transition

### Typical Communication
- User research findings and insights
- Design specification documents and prototypes
- Design review sessions with product and development teams
- Usability testing reports and recommendations

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **With Product Managers:** Validates product direction and user needs
- **With Developers:** Provides design specifications and clarifications
- **With QA Lead:** Participates in usability acceptance criteria
- **With Stakeholders:** Presents design vision and user impact

---

## Scrum Master / Delivery Lead

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, remove team blockers, and coach teams on agile practices. They enable the team to maintain velocity and continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, and retrospectives
- Identify and help remove team blockers and impediments
- Coach team on agile best practices and ceremonies
- Track sprint velocity and team metrics
- Support continuous improvement initiatives
- Shield team from external interruptions during sprint

### Goals
- Maintain consistent team velocity and delivery cadence
- Foster psychological safety and team collaboration
- Enable sustainable, predictable delivery pace

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and ceremony facilitation
- Impediment tracking and escalation
- Sprint metrics and retrospective notes
- Team coaching and process improvement discussions

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **With Project Managers:** Coordinates schedule and ceremony timing
- **With Developers:** Removes blockers and facilitates collaboration
- **With Product Managers:** Coordinates backlog refinement and prioritization
- **With All Teams:** Facilitates cross-functional ceremonies and communication

---

## Stakeholder / Sponsor

### Role Summary
Stakeholders and Sponsors set business priorities, provide approvals, and communicate with executive leadership. They ensure projects align with business goals and secure necessary resources.

### Responsibilities
- Define business objectives and success criteria
- Prioritize projects and initiatives
- Provide approvals at key decision gates
- Communicate project status to executive leadership
- Escalate business-critical risks and blockers
- Allocate budget and resources
- Participate in project kickoffs and reviews

### Goals
- Align projects with business strategy
- Maximize business impact and ROI
- Enable executive visibility and stakeholder confidence

### Typical Communication
- Project approvals and go/no-go decisions
- Executive status reports and dashboards
- Stakeholder review meetings and demos
- Business priority and trade-off discussions

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **With Project Managers:** Receives status updates and approves decisions
- **With Product Managers:** Reviews business impact and roadmap
- **With Developers:** Participates in demos and acceptance validation
- **With All Teams:** Provides business context and success metrics

---

## Security / Compliance Officer

### Role Summary
Security and Compliance Officers ensure projects meet security standards and regulatory requirements. They review designs, validate security controls, and coordinate with security scanning and incident response.

### Responsibilities
- Review designs and implementations for security risks
- Define security and compliance requirements
- Coordinate security scanning and penetration testing
- Validate third-party libraries and dependencies
- Ensure compliance with data protection and privacy standards
- Support incident response and security investigations
- Provide security training and guidance to teams

### Goals
- Reduce security vulnerabilities and compliance violations
- Maintain customer trust and regulatory standing
- Enable secure-by-design practices across projects

### Typical Communication
- Security design reviews and threat assessments
- Compliance requirement documents and checklists
- Security scanning results and remediation plans
- Incident response and post-incident reports

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **With Technical Lead:** Reviews architecture for security risks
- **With Developers:** Provides security guidance and code review
- **With QA Lead:** Incorporates security testing in test plans
- **With DevOps:** Validates deployment security controls

---

## DevOps / Infrastructure Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers manage deployment pipelines, infrastructure, and production readiness. They enable reliable, automated deployments and maintain system health and observability.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure, environments, and deployment automation
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting
- Coordinate production deployments and rollbacks
- Ensure infrastructure security and compliance
- Support incident response and system troubleshooting
- Optimize infrastructure performance and costs

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable deployments with minimal manual effort
- Maintain high system availability and performance
- Reduce deployment risk through automation and observability

### Typical Communication
- Deployment plans and release coordination
- Infrastructure change requests and reviews
- System monitoring dashboards and alerts
- Incident response and post-incident reports

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **With Project Managers:** Coordinates deployment windows and timelines
- **With Developers:** Supports environment setup and deployment troubleshooting
- **With QA Lead:** Provides test environment management and validation
- **With Security Officer:** Ensures security in infrastructure and deployments

---

## Cross-Functional Role Interactions

These personas work most effectively when:
- **Clear ownership** is established for each activity (who decides, who executes, who validates)
- **Communication channels** are defined and used consistently
- **Decision-making authority** is understood and respected
- **Dependencies** are identified and managed proactively
- **Feedback loops** enable continuous improvement
